Service Details \ Group Loan & Individual Loan

Ojas Microfinance values your dreams, and ambitions. To help you fulfil them, we bring you a variety of loans at attractive interest rates, and also make it easy for you to avail them. You can choose the one that best fits your need, and suits your convenience.

Group Loan & Individual Loan offering by Ojas Microfinance is designed to help our customers engaged in Farming/Allied Agricultural, Agri Allied activities to maintain or expand their existing set-up.

FEATURES & BENEFITS

    Loan Amount
  • Minimum amount is INR 5,000
  • Maximum amount is INR 2.6 lakhs

    Insurance Facility for Individual Loan
  • Credit Life Insurance

    Attractive Interest Rates
  • Enjoy highly competitive interest rates of 24% - 26% p.a.

    Loan Tenure
  • The tenture of the loan is in the range of 6 to 24 months.

Doorstep Banking Service

Doorstep service at every stage:
  • Onboarding
  • Loan disbursement
  • Collection
  • Servicing

Process

  • Eligibility Criteria
    Existing customers of Ojas Microfinance.
    • Customers new to Ojas Microfinance and involved in agricultural activities.
    • Applicants should be residents of the current village / city for minimum 2 years and have an agricultural set-up.
  • How to Apply
    • Visit the nearest branch of Ojas Microfinance, and meet our Loan Executive to understand the application process.
    • You can also contact us and our Customer Care Executive will fix a doorstep service appointment with our Loan Executive to help you with the application process.
  • Required Documents
    • Primary ID: Aadhaar
    • Secondary ID: PAN card / Form 60
    In case of non-availability of PAN card, customer needs to submit Form 60 along with the one of the following OVDs (Officially Valid Documents)
    • Passport
    • Voter ID card
    • Driving licence
    • NREGA (National Rural Employment Guarantee Act)
    • Letter issued by National Population Register containing details of name and address
    Residence Proof:
    • Any of the valid KYC documents
    • Utility bill (not more than 60 days old)
