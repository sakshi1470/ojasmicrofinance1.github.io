What We Do?

What
We Do?

Ojas Micro finance plays an important role in country’s economic development and inclusive growth by providing financial relief to borrowers present at the bottom of the economic pyramid.
Working on the principals of Joint liability Group not only promotes mutual accountability but overall community upliftment.

Micro finance sector has been instrumental in developing micro entrepreneurs by offering stimulus to dreams of rural women through formal credit and financial literacy.
